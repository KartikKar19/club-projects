Tara Ma

VELLORE,INDIA

Rating: 4.7/5

Type: North Indian Restaurant

Timing: : 07:00 am to 11:00 am and 12:30 pm to 10:00 pm

Address: Opp: VIT, Vaibhav Nagar Katpadi, Vellore

Highlights: One of the best to have North Indian dishes

Must haves: Ramba chicken, Cream Paneer, Dal Tadka & Rice Tara Maa.

Average Cost - Rs.1500/- for 10-15 people

The go to spot for when you either need some hearty north indian food or for when you're desperately missing ghar ka khana, Tara maa is a spot you go with your friends to for some great food at reasonable prices. The bustling noise from the shuffling of crowd to the loud screams of orders amongst the waiters and the constant long queue outside the restaurant, Tara Maa embodies the very essence of a hearty eatery where you can dine with your friends. Going on the weekends may require having to wait long hours outside the restaurant but the food is completely worth it.

Review: Amazing quality food with proper quantity and at a cheap student friendly price. Mouth watering non-veg menu at so economy prices. Highly recommended Ramba Chicken. Plenty vegetarian options are also available.

Visited during December 2022
