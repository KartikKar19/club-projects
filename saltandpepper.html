Salt & Pepper

VELLORE,INDIA

Rating: 4.7/5

Timing: 08:00 am to 11:00 pm

Address: VIT Guest House in VIT University, Vellore

Cuisines - serves Arabian & Kerala dishes

Highlights: Nice ambience & friendly customer service

Must haves: Alphahem, chicken Mandi, Puttu, tea's.

Average Cost - Rs.350/- per couple

Bringing the culture, food and ambience from god's own country, Salt and pepper is a must try for every malayali and their friend. Salt and pepper dishes out curries containing the authentic kerala spice mix and along with the posters and taglines pasted all over the walls, the place is an ode to the pop culture prevalent in the state and is guaranteed to bring back nostalgie for every mallu on campus. Like the state itself the restaurant offers a variety of sea food dishes as well as the famous kerala porotta and alfam. Puttu and ghee rice for those who miss home a little too much and for those wanted to try something new, the place offers it all at an affordable reasonable price point.

Review: Located within the University and is an authentic Arabian eatery serving a wide variety of sumptuous dishes in University Compound. It serves best combo of Arabian and Kerala dishes
