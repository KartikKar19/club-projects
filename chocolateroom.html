CHOCOLATE ROOM

VELLORE,INDIA

Rating:4/5

Type: CHOCOLATE CAFE

Timing: 11:00 am to 10:30 pm

Address: Opp: VIT, Main Gate, Daniel Nagar, Katpadi Vellore

Highlights: Seating available ambience is so mesmerizing

Must haves: Chocolate room. Browney Sundae, Death by Chocolate, Chocolate bomb

Average Cost - Rs.500/- per couple

Cute cozy and gorgeous, this place can double as a perfect date spot and as an apt spot for celebrating a special occasion. Their brownie as well as the chocolate shots are sure to cause you sugar rush but will keep you wanting more. Chocolate fondue as well as their savoury menu are something to explored as well. Kinds pricey but not really, chocolate room has probably one of the most aesthetic decors in restaurants around the campus.
